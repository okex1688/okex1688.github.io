How to Withdraw USD via SWIFT

 Trading FAQs    |      2021-09-08
You can follow the instructions below to withdraw your USD from Binance via SWIFT:
1. Log in to your Binance account and go to [Wallet] - [Fiat and Spot].
2. Click [Withdraw].
3. Under the [Fiat] tab, select [USD] - [Bank transfer (SWIFT)] and enter the withdrawal amount. Click [Continue] to create a withdrawal request.
4. Enter the account details (Your name will be filled automatically on the [Receiver Name]).
5. Check the details and confirm the withdrawal.
Once confirmed, please wait patiently. It might take up to 4 business days for your withdrawal to reach your bank account.
For more details on SWIFT transfer, please refer to:
  • How to Deposit USD via SWIFT
  • Frequently Asked Questions on Depositing and Withdrawing USD via SWIFT
