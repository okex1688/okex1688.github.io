How to Use Binance Lite Referral

 Trading FAQs    |      2022-03-28

How to Use Binance Lite Referral

Experience a new way of earning rewards by inviting your friends to join Binance. When the friend you invited registers for a Binance account and makes a deposit worth more than $50, you’ll both receive 100 USDT in trading fee credit.

1. How to earn referral rewards?

Step 1: Select [Lite Referral] mode and share your referral link with friends.
Please Note: The Lite Referral rewards cannot be earned on top of the Standard Referral. To earn rewards from our regular commission-based referral program, please choose [Standard Referral] instead.
Step 2: Once your friend registers a Binance account, completes Identity Verification, and makes a deposit worth more than $50 within 14 days after registration, you’ll both receive a 100 USDT trading fee cashback voucher.
Your friend can make their deposit using any of the following channels:
  • Cash deposit
  • Buy crypto with debit/credit card
  • P2P trading
  • Deposit crypto
Please note that any deposit via a third-party payment method or from another Binance account will not be counted towards the deposit volume.

2. How to claim the rewards?

To be eligible for the referral rewards, you must complete Identity verification within 7 days after the reward is available. Once you complete Identity Verification, it will be automatically distributed to your [Reward Center] within 48 hours. Please note that the unclaimed rewards will expire in 7 days.
If you haven’t completed Identity Verification within 7 days, only your friend will be eligible to receive the reward.
For example:
User A invites User B via Lite Referral.
User B completes the qualified deposit and has completed Identity Verification.
User A has not completed Verification, so only User B is eligible for the reward.
Once User A completes Identity Verification, User A will also be eligible for the reward.

3. How to use the cashback voucher?

After receiving the cashback voucher, go to [Reward Center] to redeem it within 7 days. Please note that all the cashback vouchers are valid for 14 days once activated. Following activation, the cashback earned from trading fees will be distributed to the user’s spot account in USDT before 23:59:59 on the next day of the user’s trading.
For more details on how to use cashback vouchers, please refer to Cashback Voucher Terms and Conditions.

4. How many trading fee cashback vouchers can I get?

There are no restrictions on the number of trading fee cashback vouchers you can earn.

5. How can I get more rewards?

You can earn more trading fee cashback vouchers by inviting more friends to join Binance and complete their first deposit.

Terms and Conditions

1. Each new Binance user can only be referred via one Referral mode. If a new user registers a Binance account via the Lite Referral ID/link, the user who invited them will not be eligible for any commission-based rewards from the Standard Referral Mode.
2. Eligible rewards can be unlocked within 7 days after distribution. The cashback voucher will expire after 7 days and the rewards cannot be claimed.
3. All rewards are distributed in cashback vouchers. To redeem, please go to [Reward Center]. Cashback vouchers will be valid for 14 days once activated.
4. Sub-accounts cannot be used to participate in this event. Deposits made via a sub-account or account transfer will not be qualified.
5. Any deposit via a third-party payment method or from another Binance account will not be counted towards the deposit volume.
6. Users need to complete Identity Verification within 7 days after the reward is available for redemption. The reward will be automatically distributed to the [Reward Center] within 48 hours after they complete Identity Verification.
7. Binance will use the real-time fiat currency to USD exchange rate to calculate the deposit and trading amounts in USD.
8. Binance reserves the right to disqualify and revoke rewards from participants who engage in dishonest or abusive activities during the activity. This includes bulk-account registrations to obtain additional bonuses and any other activity in connection with unlawful, fraudulent, or harmful purposes.
9. Binance reserves the right to cancel or amend any Activity or Activity Rules at our sole discretion.
10. All participants must strictly abide by the Binance Terms of Use.
11. Binance reserves the right of final interpretation of this activity. If you have any questions, please contact customer service.
