Auto-Invest Adds HOOK: Complete a Quiz to Receive a Free Auto-Invest HOOK Trial Plan

 Binance Announcements    |      2023-03-13
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Promotion Period: 2023-03-13 09:00 (UTC) to 2023-03-26 23:59 (UTC)
To celebrate the launch of HOOK on Auto-Invest, Binance is launching the Auto-Invest HOOK Quiz Activity at 2023-03-13 09:00 (UTC), where users can gain knowledge about Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA), Auto-Invest and HOOK. The first 50,000 new Auto-Invest users who successfully complete the quiz during the promotion period, will be eligible to each receive a free Auto-Invest HOOK monthly plan for five months.
[Click Here to Join the Promotion]
How to Get Started:
  • Step 1: Click the [Start the quiz] button at the top of the activity page.
  • Step 2: Open the quiz and read through the available resources on DCA, Auto-Invest and HOOK.
  • Step 3: Correctly answer all the questions in the quiz. Users may attempt the quiz again if they did not answer all the questions correctly.
  • Step 4: Qualified users will automatically receive their Auto-Invest HOOK monthly plans by 2023-04-09 23:59 (UTC).
Please Note:
  • The free Auto-Invest HOOK monthly plan functions as a trial plan, which allows users to experience the different features on Auto-Invest (available in your region) without incurring any costs for the first five months.
  • Eligible users will each receive an Auto-Invest HOOK monthly plan that is worth 1 USDT. This 1 USDT amount will be equally split over a period of five months to purchase HOOK via the Auto-Invest monthly plan (i.e., 0.2 USDT per installment).
  • Users may not edit, pause or cancel the Auto-Invest HOOK monthly plan until the end of the five-month period.
  • Those who do not opt out of the Auto-Invest HOOK monthly plan after the end of the five-month period will continue to purchase HOOK via the monthly plan, where 0.2 USDT will be automatically deducted from their stablecoin balances in Spot Wallets on a monthly basis.
Terms & Conditions:
  • Only new Auto-Invest users will be eligible for rewards in this promotion.
  • Users must complete account verification to successfully participate in the promotion.
  • Users need to answer all the questions in the quiz correctly to qualify for rewards. Users may attempt the quiz multiple times until they correctly answer all the questions.
  • Rewards are on a first-come, first-served basis and a maximum of 50,000 eligible participants will each receive an Auto-Invest HOOK monthly plan, which will last for a minimum of five months.
  • Illegally bulk registered accounts and sub-accounts shall not be eligible to participate or receive any rewards.
  • Binance reserves the right to disqualify any participants showing signs of fraudulent behavior immediately.
  • Binance reserves the right at any time in its sole and absolute discretion to determine and/or amend or vary these Promotion Terms without prior notice, including but not limited to canceling, extending, terminating or suspending this Promotion, its eligibility terms and criteria, the selection and number of winners, and the timing of any act to be done, and all users shall be bound by these amendments.
  • Additional promotion terms and conditions can be accessed here.
  • Where any discrepancy arises between the translated versions and the original English version, the English version shall prevail.
