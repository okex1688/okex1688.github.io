Binance Fan Token Launches FC Shakhtar Fanverse

 Binance Announcements    |      2023-04-04
Fellow Binancians,
Binance Fan Token is excited to launch the FC Shakhtar Fanverse in collaboration with FC Shakhtar. This is an all-new platform designed for FC Shakhtar fans, where users will be able to participate in various fan engagement activities, enjoy early access to exclusive NFT drops and discounts, and receive one-of-a-kind fan experiences.
As a start, users can participate in different activities at the FC Shakhtar Fanverse to share up to 36,000 USDT in token voucher rewards. More fan rewards, such as FC Shakhtar jerseys and tickets to FC Shakhtar matches, are also up for grabs from now till June 2023.
To enjoy the activities and rewards available in the FC Shakhtar Fanverse, users are required to claim a Fanverse Passport at the FC Shakhtar Fanverse from 2023-04-04 10:00 (UTC) to 2023-06-04 14:00 (UTC).
Explore the FC Shakhtar Fanverse Now!
How to Get a Fanverse Passport?
All Binance users will be eligible to claim an Experience Passport at the FC Shakhtar Fanverse, which will qualify them for one entry to any free Match Prediction challenge in the fanverse. Users holding the Experience Passport will also receive two votes at the Voting Polls, which they can participate in to influence their favorite team’s team decisions.
To receive full access to both the free Match Prediction and Voting Poll features, users may upgrade their Experience Passport to a Super Passport immediately by referring at least one new user with their unique FC Shakhtar Fanverse invitation link, and ensuring that their referral completes identity verification successfully. New users may also register with Binance through the FC Shakhtar Fanverse and complete identity verification to automatically receive the Super Passport.
What Are the Rewards?
1) Free Match Predictions: Showcase Your Sports Knowledge & Share Up to 36,000 USDT in Token Voucher Rewards
Starting from 2023-04-04 10:00 (UTC), users who hold a Fanverse Passport will be eligible to participate in Match Prediction challenges for free and earn more rewards.
With 12 FC Shakhtar matches taking place from 2023-04-08 to 2023-06-03, all participants will stand to share up to 36,000 USDT in token voucher rewards. Users will be able to submit their entry for the corresponding Match Prediction challenge at least three days before the match begins until the match’s start time.
The rewards pool for each Match Prediction challenge goes up to 3,000 USDT in token vouchers, depending on the number of participants in the activity.
Dynamic Rewards Pool for Each Match Prediction Challenge:
Rewards Structure:
For each Match Prediction challenge, users can obtain points by choosing the correct scores and outcomes for the respective match, and receive a rewards multiplier on the points obtained by inviting more friends to Binance with their unique FC Shakhtar Fanverse invitation link. The higher the number of points achieved, the greater the rewards!

Rewards Multiplier
Rewards Calculation for Each Match Prediction Challenge:
The reward received by each qualified user = [(Number of points obtained for the Match Prediction challenge x Rewards Multiplier) / Total number of points obtained for the Match Prediction challenge by all participants in the same user group*] x Total rewards pool unlocked
Please Note:
  • Those who submit the correct scores and match outcome in each Match Prediction challenge will share the unlocked rewards pool first, following the above rewards calculation mechanism. For this user group, rewards are capped at 100 USDT in token voucher per user.
  • If the unlocked rewards pool is not fully distributed to users who have submitted the correct scores and match outcome, users who predict the correct match outcome only will be qualified to share the remaining rewards according to the same formula above, capped at 50 USDT in token voucher per user.
  • Users who did not submit the correct scores or match outcomes in their entry to the Match Prediction challenge, will not qualify for any rewards.
  • Users must ensure that their referrals complete registration with Binance via their unique FC Shakhtar Fanverse invitation link and complete account verification to count as a successful referral**. Binance Fan Token will start recording each user’s number of successful referrals from 2023-04-04 10:00 (UTC), and the corresponding rewards multiplier they qualify for by each match’s starting time will be applied in the calculation of rewards, where applicable.
  • The FC Shakhtar match schedule and corresponding Match Prediction challenges may be subject to change due to force majeure and other unforeseen factors. Please refer to the FC Shakhtar Fanverse for the latest Match Predictions schedule.
2) Global Leaderboard Rewards - Rank Amongst the Top 100 Users to Receive Exclusive Fan Rewards!
Users may also receive exclusive fan rewards by obtaining points from Match Prediction challenges. The Global Leaderboard will display the total points accumulated by users from 2023-04-04 10:00 (UTC), which is inclusive of the reward multipliers they qualify for at each match’s starting time.
The final user rankings of the Global Leaderboard will be public at 2023-06-05 14:00 (UTC). If two or more users obtain the same number of points, the user who claims their Fanverse Passport earlier will receive a higher ranking at the Global Leaderboard.
More rewards and exclusive fan privileges will be available soon at the FC Shakhtar Fanverse. Stay tuned to Binance Fan Token’s Twitter, as well as FC Shakhtar’s official Twitter for the latest updates on the FC Shakhtar Fanverse!
