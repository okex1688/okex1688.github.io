Refer Friends to Get 100 USDT in Trading Fee Rebate Vouchers u0026 8 USDT in Gift Card!

 Binance Announcements    |      2023-04-10
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Binance Lite Referral is launching a brand new promotion for users who invite their friends to join Binance! Participants and their referred friends will each receive a 100 USDT Trading Fee Rebate Voucher after the latter successfully completes the deposit task. In addition, referrers may claim an 8 USDT Gift Card upon successfully referring at least three friends who sign up via their Lite Referral ID/Links and complete the deposit task.
Confirm Your Participation to Start Earning Rewards!
Promotion Period: 2023-04-10 07:00 (UTC) to 2023-04-30 23:59 (UTC)
How to Participate:
  • Visit the activity page to click the [Sign Up] button to confirm your participation, and share your Lite Referral ID/Link with a friend who has not registered with Binance. Ensure that they complete identity verification.
  • For both referrers and referrals to be eligible for the 100 USDT Trading Fee Rebate Voucher rewards, referrals must successfully complete deposits totalling more than $50 equivalent within 14 days after registration via Cash Deposit, Buy with Credit/Debit Card, P2P Trading or Cryptocurrency Deposit during the Promotion Period.
  • Referrers will qualify to receive a 100 USDT Trading Fee Rebate Voucher for each Lite Referral who successfully completes the task. An additional 8 USDT in Gift Card will be awarded to qualified referrers if they have at least three successful Lite Referrals who complete the deposit task during the Promotion Period.
Notes:
  • Both referrers and referrals may only unlock the 100 USDT Trading Fee Rebate Voucher after referrals successfully complete deposits worth a total of more than $50 equivalent within 14 days after registration. Click here to learn more details about the Lite Referral deposit task.
  • Gift Card rewards are limited and awarded on a first-come, first-served basis based on the order of registration at the activity page. A total of 10,000 USDT in Gift Card rewards are available.
Terms & Conditions:
  • Both referrers and referrals must complete KYC within seven days after the Trading Fee Rebate Voucher is available, for any party to redeem the corresponding rewards.
  • Referrers must complete KYC within seven days after the Promotion ends to claim Gift Card rewards.
  • Each new user can only be referred to Binance via one referral mode. If a new user registers a Binance account via the Lite Referral ID/Link, the referrer will not be eligible for any commission-based rewards from the Standard Referral Mode.
  • Any deposit via a third-party payment method or from another Binance account will not count toward the referral’s deposit task requirement.
  • Sub-accounts cannot be used to participate in this Promotion. Deposits made via a sub-account or account transfer will not count toward the deposit requirement.
  • While referrers may redeem one 100 USDT Trading Fee Rebate Voucher reward for every referral who successfully completes the deposit task, referrers may only claim one Gift Card reward during the Promotion Period.
  • Rewards Distribution:
    • Both referrers and referrals may redeem the 100 USDT Trading Fee Rebate Voucher via Account > Reward Center within 48 hours after referrals successfully fulfill the deposit requirement.
    • Eligible users must claim their rewards within seven days after the rewards are distributed.
    • Gift Card rewards will be distributed to winners within 20 working days after the Promotion ends.
    • Users can claim their Gift Cards on the Binance App or website. To redeem by Binance App, visit the Homepage > Profile > Gift Card. Please make sure your Binance App is updated to iOS 2.32.0 or Android 1.43.0, or later.
  • Binance will use the real-time fiat currency to USD foreign exchange rates for calculating the USD value of deposits via fiat channels to calculate the value of the deposit completed during the Promotion Period.
  • Binance reserves the right to disqualify and revoke rewards for participants who engage in dishonest or abusive activities during the Promotion. This includes bulk-account registrations to farm additional bonuses and any other activity in connection with unlawful, fraudulent, or harmful purposes.
  • Binance reserves the right at any time in its sole and absolute discretion to determine and/or amend or vary these Promotion Terms without prior notice, including but not limited to canceling, extending, terminating or suspending this Promotion, its eligibility terms and criteria, the selection and number of winners, and the timing of any act to be done, and all users shall be bound by these amendments.
  • Additional promotion terms and conditions can be accessed here.
  • All participants must strictly abide by the Binance Terms of Use.
  • Binance reserves the right of final interpretation of this Promotion.
  • Where any discrepancy arises between the translated versions and the original English version, the English version shall prevail.
Note: This announcement was last updated on 2023-04-16 to clarify that referrers must obtain at least three successful Lite Referrals who complete the deposit task during the Promotion Period to qualify for the 8 USDT Gift Card reward.
Binance reserves the right in its sole discretion to amend or cancel this announcement at any time and for any reasons without prior notice.
