Share Your Binance Academy Course Experience in Three Steps and Receive 10 USDT in Token Voucher!

 Binance Announcements    |      2023-03-07
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Fellow Binancians,

Binance Academy is pleased to announce that we have completed the launch of all six courses in the beginner track of Academy Courses! To continuously improve our educational materials, we are launching a promotion that rewards users for giving quality feedback. Let us know what you think about our courses by completing the survey and sharing on social media, and the Academy team will choose 200 users with the most constructive feedback from the survey and Twitter to share 2,000 USDT in token vouchers.
Activity Period: 2023-03-07 09:00 (UTC) to 2023-03-31 09:00 (UTC)

All KYC-verified Binance users who login to their Binance accounts and complete all of the following tasks during the activity period will stand a chance to receive 10 USDT in token voucher.

How to Get Started:
  • Step 1: Complete the six Binance Academy Beginner Courses and their corresponding quizzes.
  • Step 2: Share your learning experience on Twitter with the hashtags #BinanceAcademy and #AcademyCourses. Please ensure that your Twitter account or tweet is set to public.
  • Step 3: Complete the survey.
Start Learning Now

  • NFT certificates received upon completion of Academy Courses are non-tradable. Users are not allowed to list or trade NFT certificates from Academy Courses on the Binance NFT Marketplace.
  • NFT certificates for each learning track will be distributed to eligible users within 14 days of them passing the respective quizzes. Users can view their NFT certificates by visiting Binance NFT > Profile > Collected NFTs.
