Join Binance Pay’s $1 Game for a Chance to Receive Up to 245,000 TROY in Rewards!

 Binance Announcements    |      2023-03-17
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Fellow Binancians,
Binance is pleased to launch the next round of Binance Pay’s $1 Game, where one eligible user will qualify to receive a 245,000 TROY token voucher, and 70,000 eligible users will qualify to each receive 30 TROY in rewards.
Activity Period: 2023-03-17 12:00 (UTC) until 2023-03-24 01:59 (UTC)
How to Participate:
  1. Tap on the top-right corner of the Binance App homepage to access Binance Pay, and tap on the [$1 Game] icon.
  2. Complete the quiz with the correct answer.
  3. Tap [Play Now] and submit a bid for the $1 Game by paying $1 from your Funding Wallet to receive one ticket.
  4. Click the [Invite Friends] button to receive more tickets. Each user may get up to three tickets by inviting one user to successfully participate in the game, and a maximum of 30 tickets for each round of the $1 Game.
The more tickets a user has, the more likely it is for the user to win the bid. New Binance users must participate in the $1 Game within 14 days from their registration date to count as a successful invitee.
Depending on when the user participates in the game, inviters may receive the corresponding tickets during the current $1 Game during the Activity Period, or during the next weekly iteration.
  1. Users will receive an app push/in-app notification confirming their participation. To view your transaction details, proceed to the Binance App > Binance Pay > [My Subscription].
Rewards Structure:
  • Grand Prize:
    • One eligible user who participates in Binance Pay’s $1 Game during the Activity Period will be selected based on the Award Selection Rules to receive a 245,000 TROY token voucher.
    • Binance Pay will announce the results of the game and distribute the Grand Prize to the winner within 24 hours after the Activity ends.
  • Bonus Rewards:
    • 70,000 eligible users who participate in Binance Pay’s $1 Game during the Activity Period will be selected based on the Award Selection Rules to each receive 30 TROY in the form of a Locked Trial Fund Voucher.
    • A 150-day TROY Locked Product position will be created for eligible users once the Locked Trial Fund Voucher is redeemed, which allows users to enjoy 10% in APR rewards via Simple Earn Locked Products. APR rewards will be distributed directly to users’ Spot Wallets.
    • Upon expiry of the Locked Product position created with the TROY Locked Trial Fund, Binance Simple Earn will transfer the principal amount of the Fund to Simple Earn Flexible Products. Learn more about Locked Trial Fund Vouchers here.
All users who do not hold the winning ticket for the Grand Prize, including those who qualify for Bonus Rewards, will receive a refund of the $1 bidding amount in their Funding Wallet within 48 hours after the Activity ends.
Participate in Binance Pay’s $1 Game Now!
Stay tuned to the $1 Game page on the Binance App for more information on the next round of the $1 Game.

Terms & Conditions
  • To access Binance Pay, download the latest Binance App, or upgrade your Binance App to iOS v2.47.0 or Android v2.47.0, or later.
  • An eligible user is defined as a verified user who has successfully registered for a Binance Pay account.
  • This is a non-purchase promotion. Users who do not receive the Grand Prize will be refunded 1 BUSD within 48 hours after the Activity ends.
  • The $1 bidding amount will be denominated in BUSD. Users who do not have sufficient BUSD in their Funding Wallet may choose another token in their possession to convert to an equivalent of 1 BUSD.
  • Each user will be eligible for a maximum of one reward only. The Grand Prize and Bonus Rewards are mutually exclusive.
  • Eligible users may obtain one ticket for the $1 Game by locking in the $1 bidding amount. Additional tickets can only be obtained via inviting friends to join the $1 Game.
  • Users cannot cancel their bid in the $1 Game once it is placed. After authorizing the bid, the bidding amount (i.e., $1) will be locked until the end of the Activity Period.
  • Grand Prize:
    • The winner will be notified via an app push/in-app notification within 24 hours after the Activity ends. Please note that the winner will not receive a refund of the bid that he/she has submitted.
    • The 245,000 TROY token voucher will be distributed within 24 hours after the Activity ends. The winner will be able to view the token voucher reward via Account > Reward Center.
    • The token voucher is valid for 30 days from the date of distribution. Learn how to redeem a voucher.
  • Bonus Rewards:
    • Locked Trial Fund Voucher rewards will be distributed to eligible users’ Reward Center within 14 days after the promotion ends. Once distributed, users will have 14 days to redeem the voucher.
    • A 150-day TROY Locked Product position will be created for eligible users once the Locked Trial Fund Voucher is redeemed, which allows users to enjoy 10% in APR rewards via Simple Earn Locked Products.
    • APR rewards will be distributed to users’ Spot Wallets. Users can view their assets on Simple Earn Locked Products by going to Wallets > Earn > Simple Earn > Locked.
    • Upon expiry of the Locked Product position created with the TROY Locked Trial Fund, Binance Simple Earn will transfer the principal amount of the Fund to Simple Earn Flexible Products. Learn more about Locked Trial Fund Vouchers here.
    • For this Activity, users may not redeem their digital assets/digital currencies in advance.
    • All Bonus Reward recipients will receive a refund of $1 in their Funding Wallet within 48 hours after the Activity ends.

    • Sell, buy or trade user accounts or impersonate any other individual in connection with user accounts, and the foregoing shall include all circumventions, similar actions or actions that produce an effect that matches the aforementioned prohibitions.
