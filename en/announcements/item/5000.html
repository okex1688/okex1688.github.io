Complete Higher Dual Investment Subscriptions u0026 Get Up to 2,000 USDT in Token Voucher

 Binance Announcements    |      2023-04-10
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Promotion Period: 2023-04-11 00:00 (UTC) - 2023-04-24 23:59 (UTC)
Binance is delighted to launch a new promotion for Dual Investment users, where both regular and VIP users will stand to each receive up to 2,000 USDT in token vouchers. Users who participate in this promotion may also qualify for the VIP & Institutional Investors Program for Dual Investment users, which extends VIP benefits to all eligible Dual Investment users.
[Click Here to Join the Promotion]
How to Qualify for Rewards:
All eligible users who opt-in to the promotion can stand to receive up to 2,000 USDT in token vouchers each, depending on their Net Dual Investment Subscription Amount.
Each user’s Net Dual Investment Subscription Amount is calculated with their total Dual Investment subscription amounts before and during the Promotion Period, as follows:
  • Total Dual Investment Subscription Amount between 2023-04-11 00:00 (UTC) and 2023-04-24 23:59 (UTC) minus Total Dual Investment Subscription Amount between 2023-03-28 00:00 (UTC) and 2023-04-10 23:59 (UTC)
Rewards Structure:
Calculation Example for Net Dual Investment Subscription Amount:
  • Between 2023-03-28 00:00 (UTC) and 2023-04-10 23:59 (UTC), a user makes two Dual Investment subscriptions of $100,000 equivalent each, reaching a total Dual Investment subscription amount of $200,000 equivalent.
  • During the Promotion Period, the user makes five Dual Investment subscriptions of $100,000 equivalent each, and reaches a total Dual Investment subscription amount of $500,000 equivalent.
  • Therefore, the user’s Net Dual Investment Subscription Amount is $500,000 minus $200,000, i.e., $300,000 equivalent.
  • The user will then qualify to receive a 200 USDT token voucher, based on the rewards structure above.
How to Participate:
  • Step 1: Click on the [Join Promotion] button on the activity page.
  • Step 2: Subscribe to any Dual Investment product.
  • Step 3: Complete higher Dual Investment subscriptions to qualify for a higher voucher amount.
