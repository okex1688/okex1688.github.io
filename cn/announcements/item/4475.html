Trade on USDT/PLN Spot Trading Pair u0026 Win Up to 1,000 USDT

 最新公告    |      2023-04-14
To celebrate the launch of the new USDT/PLN spot trading pair, Binance is presenting two promotions, where eligible users stand a chance to receive 5 USDT per day, and win up to 1,000 USDT by trading on the USDT/PLN spot trading pair during the promotion period.
Promotion Period: 2023-04-14 08:00 (UTC) - 2023-05-05 07:59 (UTC)
With the introduction of the USDT/PLN spot trading pair, users can now trade PLN on BTC/PLN, ETH/PLN, BUSD/PLN, and USDT/PLN spot trading pairs.
Promotion A: Trade & Claim 5 USDT Daily
The first 50 verified users who trade at least a total of $400 equivalent (including buys and sells) on the USDT/PLN spot trading pair on the day during the promotion period, will be eligible to claim a 5 USDT token voucher for that day through the Task Center.
Eligible users may complete the task on multiple days to receive multiple rewards.
Trade on USDT/PLN Spot Trading Pair to Earn 5 USDT Daily!
Promotion B: Top Three Users to Share 1,750 USDT
The top three traders ranked by their total trading volume on the USDT/PLN spot trading pair during the promotion period, will be eligible to share a prize pool of 1,750 USDT in token vouchers.
Rewards Structure:
  • 1st Place: 1,000 USDT
  • 2nd Place: 500 USDT
  • 3rd Place: 250 USDT
Trade on USDT/PLN Spot Trading Pair to Win 1,000 USDT!
Terms & Conditions
  • To qualify for the promotions, users should complete the KYC process on Binance.
  • Promotion A:
    • Only users who are verified as residents of Poland will be able to access the task through the Task Center.
    • Newly verified users will be eligible to access the task one day after the KYC process is completed. Please note it may take at least 24 hours to complete the KYC process.
    • Users may participate in Promotion A repeatedly and win multiple rewards on multiple days. The number of rewards is limited to 50 per day. A user may win a maximum of 5 USDT in token voucher daily.
    • For the purpose of distributing rewards in Promotion A, each day runs from 08:00 (UTC) and ends at 07:59 (UTC) of the next day.
    • Binance will use the closing price of PLN to USD exchange rate as of 2023-04-14 on National Bank of Poland (NBP) for calculating the trade volume requirements.
    • Token voucher rewards will be distributed within ten days after the promotion ends. You will be able to log in and redeem your token voucher via Account > Reward Center.
    • The validity period for the token voucher is set at 14 days from the day of distribution. Learn how to redeem a token voucher.
  • Promotion B:
    • One user is eligible to receive one reward only.
    • Token voucher rewards will be distributed within 14 days after the promotion ends. You will be able to log in and redeem your token voucher via Account > Reward Center.
    • The validity period for the token voucher is set at 14 days from the day of distribution. Learn how to redeem a token voucher.
  • The winners will be informed about their rewards via email notification and inmail.
  • Users can participate in Promotion A and Promotion B simultaneously and win rewards from both promotions.
