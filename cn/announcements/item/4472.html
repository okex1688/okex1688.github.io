Complete Your First Purchase on P2P Express u0026 Subscription to Simple Earn Locked Products to Get Up to 2.5 ERN as Rewards!

 最新公告    |      2023-04-05
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Reminder: Click the [Register Now] button on the activity page to be eligible for rewards
Binance P2P and Binance Simple Earn are excited to launch two promotions to reward our community! Users who are new to the Binance P2P Express Zone and Simple Earn Locked Products can participate in these promotions, and stand to share 5,000 ERN in Locked Trial Fund vouchers.
Promotion Period: 2023-04-06 10:00 (UTC) to 2023-04-26 10:00 (UTC)
Promotion A: Complete Your First Purchase on P2P Express to Get a 2 ERN Locked Trial Fund Voucher (Takers only)
This promotion is only open for takers who have not completed a transaction on the Binance P2P Express Zone before 2023-04-06 10:00 (UTC).
During the Promotion Period, the first 1,250 eligible users who confirm their participation in this promotion and complete one of the following tasks, can each receive a 2 ERN Locked Trial Fund Voucher and enjoy 10% in APR rewards:
  • Complete their first purchase of any cryptocurrencies worth at least $50 equivalent on the Binance P2P Express Zone while using an eligible local currency, OR
  • Complete their first purchase of any amount of ERN with an eligible local currency on Binance P2P Express Zone.
Promotion B: Subscribe to ERN Locked Products to Receive a 0.5 ERN Locked Trial Fund Voucher
This promotion is only open for users who have never subscribed to Simple Earn Locked Products before 2023-04-06 10:00 (UTC). During the Promotion Period, the first 5,000 users who confirm their participation in this promotion and complete a subscription to ERN Locked Products, will qualify to each receive a 0.5 ERN Locked Trial Fund Voucher and enjoy 10% in APR rewards.
Notes:
  • A 150-day ERN Locked Product position will be created for eligible users once ERN Locked Trial Fund Voucher(s) are redeemed. This entitles users to enjoy 10% in APR rewards, which will be distributed to their Spot Wallets daily.
  • Upon expiry of the Locked Product position created with the ERN Locked Trial Fund, Binance Simple Earn will transfer the principal amount of the Fund to Simple Earn Flexible Products.

    • Promotion A is only applicable to takers who have not traded on Binance P2P Express Zone prior to 2023-04-06 10:00 (UTC).
    • Taker: When you place an order on Binance P2P that trades immediately before going on the order book, you are a taker. These trades are “taking” volume off the order book, and are therefore taker trades.
    • Binance will use the daily closing price at 23:59 (UTC) of the local currency to USD foreign exchange rate for the calculation of the user purchase volume requirement in Promotion A.
  • Promotion B is only applicable to users who are subscribing to Binance Simple Earn Locked Products for the first time during the Promotion Period.

    • A 150-day ERN Locked Product position will be created for eligible users once ERN Locked Trial Fund Voucher rewards from Promotion A and/or B are redeemed. This entitles users to enjoy 10% in APR rewards.
    • Upon expiry of the Locked Product position created with the ERN Locked Trial Fund, Binance Simple Earn will transfer the principal amount of the Fund to Simple Earn Flexible Products.
