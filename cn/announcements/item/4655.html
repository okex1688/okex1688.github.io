Refer Your Friends to Binance to Earn a Share of 12,500 USDT in Token Voucher Rewards

 最新公告    |      2023-03-06
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Binance is happy to announce a referral promotion for users in the Middle East and North Africa. Eligible users who invite their friends to Binance will stand a chance to share a total of 12,500 USDT in token voucher rewards with their invitees! Simply refer your friends to Binance for a chance to qualify.
Sign Up for the Promotion and Start Referring Friends Now!
Promotion Period: 2023-03-06 08:00 (UTC) to 2023-03-20 11:59 (UTC)
How to Participate:
  • Submit your Binance UID through this form.
  • Refer friends to Binance by sharing your unique referral link.
Promotion A: Refer Friends to Binance to Earn a Share of 8,000 USDT in Token Vouchers
Refer friends via the unique referral link generated from the Standard Referral Mode, and ensure that the invitees complete account verification. Users will be rewarded on a first-come, first-served basis based on the time of form submission and number of successful referrals, and be rewarded up to 100 USDT in token voucher each.
Reward Structure:
Notes:
  • Only invitees who have completed account verification count as a successful referral.
  • Token voucher rewards are capped at 100 USDT for an individual referrer.
  • Regular benefits from the Standard Referral Program apply when new users register via the unique referral link.
Promotion B: Verify Your Account as a New User to Share 2,000 USDT in Token Vouchers
The first 1,000 referred users will each receive a 2 USDT token voucher after completing account verification (KYC).
Promotion C: Top Five Users Ranked by Number of Successful Referrals to Share an Additional Pool of 2,500 USDT in Token Vouchers
All eligible users who participated in Promotion A and completed the required tasks will stand a chance to share 2,500 USDT in token vouchers based on the number of successful referrals made during the Promotion Period.

Terms and Conditions:
  • Both referrers and invitees must complete KYC in the Middle East and North Africa (excluding Israel, Bahrain and the UAE) to be eligible for the corresponding rewards.
  • A successful referral is a user who registers with the referrer’s referral link / QR code and completes account verification during the promotion period.
  • Promotion A:
    • Token voucher rewards are capped at 100 USDT for an individual referrer.
    • Users will be rewarded on a first-come, first-served basis based on the time of form submission and number of successful referrals until the rewards pool is depleted.
  • Only users invited via the Binance Standard Referral Program will be eligible for rewards.
  • Token voucher rewards will be distributed within 14 days after the promotion ends. Users will be able to log in and redeem their voucher rewards via Account > Reward Center.
  • The validity of the token voucher is set at 30 days from the date of distribution. Learn how to redeem a voucher.
