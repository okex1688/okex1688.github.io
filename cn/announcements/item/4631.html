Binance Pay Partners with Yesim: Enjoy Unlimited Data When Using the Binance App u0026 Additional Data Rewards!

 最新公告    |      2023-03-20
This is a general announcement. Products and services referred to here may not be available in your region.
Fellow Binancians,
Binance Pay is thrilled to announce our new partnership with Yesim, a top-tier provider of eSIM plans. To celebrate this, Binance Pay is launching a new promotion, where users can enjoy unlimited data while using the Binance App when they purchase and activate an eSIM plan worth at least 10 EUR equivalent using Binance Pay, via the Yesim mini program at Binance Marketplace.
On top of that, the first 10,000 users who purchase and activate an eSIM plan worth at least 10 EUR equivalent from Yesim with Binance Pay during the Promotion Period, will qualify to receive additional data on the newly-purchased eSIM plan, worth a total of 2 EUR.
Promotion Period: 2023-03-20 00:00 (UTC) until further notice
How to Participate:
  1. New users must download the Binance App and sign up for a Binance Pay account.
  2. Proceed to the Yesim mini program at the Binance Marketplace.
  3. Purchase and activate an eSIM plan from Yesim using Binance Pay. Ensure that the eSIM plan is worth at least 10 EUR equivalent to qualify for rewards.
How to Access Yesim on Binance Marketplace:
  1. Login to your Binance App, and make sure that the Pro mode is enabled. Drag down on the homepage to access [Marketplace], and find Yesim from the listed merchants.
Alternatively, users may scan the QR Code with their mobile devices or via the Binance App to access the Yesim mini program at Binance Marketplace.

  • Only eligible users who successfully purchase and activate an eSIM plan from Yesim using Binance Pay via the Yesim mini program on the Binance Marketplace during the Promotion Period, will qualify to receive unlimited data while using the Binance App. On top of that, 10,000 users who fulfill the above requirements during the Promotion Period, will receive 2 EUR worth of additional data on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Users must purchase an eSIM plan from Yesim that is worth at least 10 EUR equivalent to qualify for rewards.
