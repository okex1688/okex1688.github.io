Exchange With Kindness - Transfer Crypto Internationally to Your Loved One via Binance Gift Card to Share 3,000 USDT!

 最新公告    |      2023-03-24
Fellow Binancians,
To celebrate this joyful Ramadan, send a gift in crypto to your family members from anywhere across the globe using Binance Gift Card’s latest “Transfer Crypto'' function — completely free of charge and no recipient information required.
Transfer Crypto Now!
Head to the Gift Card Marketplace or Gift card webpage and use the “Transfer Crypto” function to share a total rewards pool of 3,000 USDT in Gift Cards! With this function, senders are able to send crypto internationally, instantly, free of charge, and only with a 16-digit code - no recipient information required.
Promotion Period: 2023-03-24 00:00 (UTC) - 2023-04-16 23:59 (UTC)
Eligible senders who use the “Transfer Crypto” function to send a minimum value of $5 equivalent during the Promotion Period will qualify to equally share a rewards pool of 3,000 USDT in Gift Cards.
Rewards Calculation: Reward for each qualified user = (1/ total number of unique eligible senders during the Promotion Period) * $3,000 in Gift Cards.
Notes:
  • Rewards are limited to the first 3,000 senders based on the code redemption time of the cryptocurrency transferred.
  • If there are 3,000 eligible senders, each eligible sender will receive $1 in Gift Card.
  • The cryptocurrency transferred using the “Transfer Crypto” function during the Promotion Period must be redeemed by 2023-04-16 23:59 (UTC) for each sender to qualify for the Promotion and receive a reward.
  • The sender and the recipient must be different users (sender and recipient cannot be the same person or UID).
Guides & Related Materials:
  • How to Generate Code and Transfer Crypto with Binance Gift Card
Terms and Conditions:
  • Each UID is limited to one reward only.
  • Only eligible senders using the “Transfer Crypto” function during the Promotion Period will be eligible for rewards.
  • The sender using the “Transfer Crypto” function cannot be the same person or UID as the recipient. Recipients must claim the cryptocurrency transferred via the “Transfer Crypto” function by 2023-04-16 23:59 (UTC) for the sender to qualify for the Promotion and receive a reward.
  • Rewards will be distributed via Gift Card to the email address bound to the user’s Binance UID by 2023-04-16 23:59 (UTC). Users who do not have an email address bound to their UID will not receive the reward.
  • All the distributed Gift Cards will expire within two weeks after distribution. Eligible users should claim their Gift Cards before the expiration date.
  • Users can claim their Gift Cards on the Binance App or website. To redeem by Binance App, visit the Homepage > Profile > Gift Card. Please make sure your Binance App is updated to iOS 2.32.0 or Android 1.43.0, or later.
  • Sub-accounts will not be viewed as independent accounts when participating in the Promotion.
  • Additional promotion terms and conditions can be accessed here.
  • Binance reserves the right to disqualify any participants that, in its reasonable opinion, are acting fraudulently or not in accordance with any applicable terms and conditions.
  • Binance reserves the right to modify or cancel any terms or conditions of the Promotion.
  • ​​Where any discrepancy arises between the translated versions and the original English version, the English version shall prevail.
